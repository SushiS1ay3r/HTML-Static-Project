New York Spots You Can't Miss!
Caroll Gardens - Lucali's (Pizza)
This is seriously the best pizza slice you will ever have in the USA and maybe even the world. Jay-Z even liked it!
However, I do warn that the wait line is more than 4 hours! If you do go there, please get the calzone, trust me.
Anyways, the restaurant is at the heart of Caroll Gardens and it has a very nice wine tasting atmosphere and
candles. You definetly need to try this for one of your bucket list!
Canal Street - Chinese Food
Oh man! This is one of my favorite Chinatown besides the Brooklyn 8th Avenue and the Queens Flushing.
I always went here with my friends and even their parents as they have some of the best authentic
Cantonese and Chinese food. From dumplings to Dim Sum to chicken feet, you should definetly visit
here anything you are feeling hungry. Plus, it is cheap!
Times Square - Entertainment & Atmosphere
This is like the hub and center attraction of New York City. When people including me think of where to go,
they always come to Times Square because it literally looks like we are 30 years into the future. Times
Square is always dazzling with its amazing and vibrant ADs, broadway entertainment, and people that never
sleep. Not to mention, Times Square is where the New Year ball drop happens yearly!
Not so great things about NYC...
  • Subways!
    • If you ever go to the subways, they are always crowded.
    • They are also a big home to the rats and sometimes smelly.
    • Worst of all, it can't handle natural disasters!
  • Crowding!
    • Times Square and Union Square are always swarming with people.
    • You might get pushed and even get lost.
    • If you lose your phone in the crowd, good luck.
  • Prices!
    • Some restaurants are charging ridiculous prices.
    • A dollar can soda from Europe can be like 5 dollars here.
    • Expensive and low quantity.
